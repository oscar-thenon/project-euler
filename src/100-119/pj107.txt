If we call $S_1,...,S_k$ the vertices that we have already met, then we find $S_1',...,S_k'$, respectively the closest vertice from $S_1,...,S_k$ (we exclude $S_1,...,S_k$ themselves from this research because we don't want to go back to them). Then we move to the $S_i'$ which minimizes the distance from $S_i$.

We continue until we have met all the vertices. At the beginning, we start from any vertice : it doesn't matter.
